Services

Tyre Retreading

Rubflex does retreading for tyre sizes 315 and 12x24. We start with the best quality casing (Bridgestone brand) and give it a new life.

Our retreaded tyres are as good as new but cost 75-80% less.

Tyre Repair

Rubflex does repairs and vulcanizing for large, heavy-duty tyres of tractors and earth-moving vehicles.

Our quality workmanship and fast turnaround times are valuable to our customers. You save time and money with Rubflex.

Radiator Repair/Servicing

Rubflex does repair and servicing of radiators of all types and sizes, especially large ones used in Caterpillar Large Diesel Generators.

We do the re-coring of radiators with the highest quality of replacement cores. Every stage of the work is tested in our factory to ensure that we deliver the highest quality always.

Brake Lining Repairs

In heavy load carrying trailer trucks, it is vital to have its brakes in optimum condition.

Rubflex ensures quality in every brake lining job we do.

Electric Motor Repair

Rubflex specializes in heavy and light duty electric motors repairs. We inspect each motor to identify all faults and then find the most cost-effective solution to fix the problems.

You can be sure about our quality of work and quick turnaround time.

Heavy Equipment Repair

Rubflex has repaired and serviced heavy equipment and parts, like hydraulic hoses of all sizes, jacks, pistons and gear-boxes. We do all associated works like supplying gaskets, and steel fabrication work with precision and cost-efficiency.

No job is too big or too small for Rubflex.
