About Rubflex

Rubflex company has been in the tyre and radiator business since the year 2000. The company has been catering to the needs of the trucking fleet owners by providing them with high quality retreaded tyres at a fraction of the cost of new tyres. We repair and service all types of radiators, hydraulic hoses, large size tyre repair and vulcanizing, pistons, jacks, electric motors, starter motors, and all kinds of heavy machinery and equipment.

Starting as a small factory in Riyadh, Sulay area, it has now established its factory in a larger space in Durma, while maintaining its after-sales-office in Riyadh Al-Mashaal Area to attend to the existing customers.
